Our story about F1

Formula 1 began in 1950 as a small European racing series and grew into the world's fastest, most technically advanced motorsport.

Every season brings new teams, new rules, and new rivalries to the grid.

From Monaco's tight streets to Singapore's night race, each circuit tells its own story.

DUM was built by fans who wanted one place to follow it all.

This is where the story continues.
